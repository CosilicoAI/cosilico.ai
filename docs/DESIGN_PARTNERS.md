# Cosilico Design Partner Targets

**Goal:** Sign 3-5 design partners before seed raise
**Timeline:** Q1 2025
**Last Updated:** November 2024

---

## Priority Tiers

### Tier 1: High Fit (Direct Use Case)
Companies that need tax/benefit calculations TODAY and would immediately use our API.

### Tier 2: Medium Fit (Adjacent Use Case)
Companies whose products would benefit from tax/benefit intelligence.

### Tier 3: Exploratory (Future Fit)
Companies building in adjacent spaces that may need this later.
